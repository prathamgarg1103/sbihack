## Slide 2 — The problem & the two wounds

- Insurance: most-complained channel — **12,000+ mis-selling complaints/yr** (SBI under pressure).
- Investing: a narrow, SBI-skewed shelf → users leave for Groww/Zerodha.
- *91% are open to transaction-based offers — the channel is broken, not the demand.*
**SAY:** "85 million users, most never go past UPI. Insurance is distrusted from mis-selling;
investing feels self-dealing. But 91% are open — it's the channel that's broken."
**VISUAL:** Iceberg (UPI tip / submerged features) + two small wound cards.

## Slide 3 — The reframe
**ON-SLIDE:** **Not cross-sell. Mis-selling remediation — disguised as an adoption engine.**

explainable** digital one. **Honesty is the moat.**
**SAY:** "We don't pitch cross-sell. We pitch mis-selling remediation — what SBI is already
scrambling to deliver — as an adoption engine. Honesty becomes the moat."
**VISUAL:** Arrow: coercive human → honest AI copilot; "Cross-sell" struck through → "Remediation".
