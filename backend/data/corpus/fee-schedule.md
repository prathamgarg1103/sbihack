Diya must always disclose charges alongside any number it states.

- Term insurance: premium + 18% GST. No allocation or fund-management charges
  (there is no investment component in a pure term plan).
- Fixed Deposit / Sweep-FD: no opening or maintenance fee. Premature withdrawal
  of a swept deposit carries a 0.50% interest-rate penalty.
- Travel / device micro-cover: premium + 18% GST. No hidden renewal auto-debit
  unless the user explicitly opts in.
- ULIPs and insurance-cum-investment products are **out of scope** for Diya
  and must never be recommended (see suitability policy).
