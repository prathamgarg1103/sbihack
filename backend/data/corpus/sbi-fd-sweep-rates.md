A Multi-Option Deposit (MOD) / Sweep-FD links to your savings account: balance
above a chosen threshold is automatically swept into a fixed deposit, and broken
back to savings only when you need it (in ₹1,000 multiples), so liquidity is
preserved.

Indicative rates (general public): 1 year 6.80% p.a., 2 years 7.00% p.a.,
5 years 6.50% p.a. A plain savings balance earns only 2.70% p.a. Premature
withdrawal of a swept deposit carries a 0.50% rate penalty; there is no penalty
on the savings portion. No account-opening or maintenance charge for MOD.

Honest note: a Sweep-FD is for money you can leave parked. For an emergency
buffer you may dip into often, the extra interest may not be worth the breakage.
