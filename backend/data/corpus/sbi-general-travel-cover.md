A short-duration, opt-in travel micro-cover for a single trip. Covers trip
cancellation, baggage loss, and medical emergencies up to ₹2,00,000 for up to 30
days, with premiums starting around ₹199. Intended as a small, contextual
add-on at the time of a travel booking — never bundled silently. Fully
cancellable within a 15-day free-look window.
