SBI Life eShield Next is a pure term insurance plan: it pays a lump sum to the
nominee if the life assured dies during the policy term. It is **not** an
investment product and carries no maturity/survival payout, which keeps the
premium low.

For a healthy 30-year-old non-smoker, a cover of ₹1,00,00,000 (1 crore) costs
about ₹11,800 per year (~₹983/month) on a regular-pay basis. The FY24 claim
settlement ratio was 99.2%. Premiums are guaranteed level for the chosen term.

Charges are limited to applicable GST on premium. There are no allocation or
fund-management charges because there is no investment component.
