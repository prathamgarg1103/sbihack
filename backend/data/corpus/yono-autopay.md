AutoPay sets a UPI / NACH e-mandate so a recurring bill or subscription pays
itself on time. Because the bank holds the mandate, you see every recurring
charge at source and can pause or stop any mandate from inside YONO — unlike a
third-party tracker that has to screen-scrape. This is the bank-native advantage
for subscription control.
