YONO has a built-in BBPS biller for electricity, water, gas, broadband and DTH.
Paying a bill inside YONO takes under 30 seconds and keeps the payment, receipt
and reminders in one place — no third-party app needed. There is no convenience
fee on BBPS utility payments. This is a feature many users never discover and
instead pay the same biller through another app.
