YONO can list the entire investable market — every AMC's mutual funds and the
full equity universe — not just SBI's own products. The figures here are
illustrative, not live quotes; returns are market-linked, can be negative, and
are not guaranteed. The point Saarthi makes: breadth is what makes honest
guidance credible. Once the whole shelf is on display, a recommendation for an
SBI fund is believed — because Saarthi will just as readily point you to a
cheaper or better-performing competitor fund. SIPs and index funds are simple,
non-ULIP products that pass the suitability filter.
