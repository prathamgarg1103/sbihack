**On-screen action:** Diya app open on the **Demo** tab, YONO-style phone shell centered, Diya
header visible. Slow, calm cursor drift or a gentle zoom-in on the phone shell. At ~0:12 briefly
pan to the **Agent Decision Loop** panel (badge "LLM live" if key is set).

**Narration (59 words):**
> "YONO has eighty-five million users — but most only ever touch UPI. The deeper features go
> undiscovered, because the channel that sells them is distrusted: over twelve thousand
> mis-selling complaints in one year. Diya is an agentic copilot that fixes the trust, not just
> the funnel — a fully autonomous perceive, reason, act, learn loop over a user's own consented
> transactions."

**Text overlays:**
- Lower third (0:00): `85M users · most never go past UPI`
- Lower third (0:14): `Diya — agentic adoption copilot · perceive → reason → act → learn`

**Proves:** the problem framing + that the agent loop is real (badge visible), per judging
criterion "Agentic AI".

  ₹1,240 — that mismatch is fine and intentional.
- If a new feature slips the build deadline, cut its scene and pad Scene 8's hold — do NOT
  compress narration to squeeze it back in; the 3:00 cap is hard.

  the text above is what a human narrator should read.
